## How to Play ğŸ®
1. **Starting the Game**:
   - Open the game in your browser.
   - Enter your name on the start screen and click **Begin Duel**.

2. **Game Controls**:
   - **W**: Move Up
   - **A**: Move Left
   - **S**: Move Down
   - **D**: Move Right

3. **Objective**:
   - **Eat Food**: Grow your snake and score points.
   - **Defeat the AI**: Use strategy and power-ups to outmaneuver and trap the AI snake.

4. **Winning and Losing**:
   - The game ends when one snake crashes into the wall, itself, or the opponent. 
   - Victory is yours if you outlive the AI or trap it!
